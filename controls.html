About DiaScape

Diascape:Escape Room as the name suggest revolves around querying around plans and puzzles to escape from a room. The game involves brain exercise of the player at mathematical and analytical level. The game forces the player into deep thinking as it involves certain levels of permutations and combinations. The player is trapped into the room and he needs to fathom several puzzles in order to procure a pass code which allow the player to exit from the room. There are several levels which the player needs to clear to win the game.

Instructions

Welcome to the Escape Room
    You will need to progress through 3 levels in order to get out of building.
    Each room will present a new challenge.
    The passcodes for each room can be alphanumeric.

Controls

All you need to use is mouse to change camera angle and mouse click to find the hidden clues in the room.
